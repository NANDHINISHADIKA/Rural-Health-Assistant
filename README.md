# 🚑 Rural Health Assistant

A multilingual AI-powered health guidance app designed for rural communities in India who have limited access to doctors and hospitals.

## 🌟 Why This App?
Millions of people in rural India face a major problem — they don't know what to do when they fall sick. Doctors are far away, internet knowledge is limited, and language is a barrier. This app solves all three problems.

## 🌐 Languages Supported
- English
- Tamil (தமிழ்)
- Hindi (हिंदी)
- Telugu (తెలుగు)
- Malayalam (മലയാളം)

## 🔍 Features
- Select symptoms in your own language
- Get possible condition name
- See severity level (Mild / Moderate / Serious)
- Get simple home remedies
- Know which hospital to visit
- Emergency numbers (108 Ambulance, 104 Health Helpline)

## 🩺 Symptoms Covered
- Fever
- Headache
- Cough
- Chest Pain
- Stomach Pain
- Vomiting
- Diabetes Symptoms
- Snake Bite
- Eye Problem
- Back Pain
- Diarrhea
